Jeff Lynne and the Electic Light Orchestra

Who is Jeff Lynne

Born in December of 1947, Jeff Lynne is the founder and lead singer of Electric Light Orchestra(ELO). He started the band in 1970 and produced 15 studio albums with the band, plus 2 solo albums in 1990 and 2012. Lynne was heavily inspired by The Beatles, which is reflected in his distinct musical style
